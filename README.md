# ViralCrime-PassionProject
www.viralcrime.com

## Elevator Pitch:
ViralCrime.com is a decentralized crime-intelligence platform that archives and verifies local crime incidents that fall through the cracks of mainstream media. It is for victims, families, and community advocates who need high-visibility, an AI-powered megaphone to bring awareness to overlooked cases.

## Problem Statement:
Mainstream outlets prioritize sensationalism, leaving thousands of "quiet" crimes (unsolved burglaries, cold cases, and local scams) without public pressure or awareness. This site exists to provide data equity, ensuring that the lack of a news van doesn't mean a lack of justice or exposure. People often spread (mis)information on local crime on social media, and this site would seek to be the source of truth using user submissions/testimony, scrubbing official municipal records, and AI fact-checking technology.

## Target User By Role:
"Bystander" - someone just viewing the site for journalism/informational purposes. Site guest.
  ex. A concerned citizen looking to get details on a local police incident.
"Community Reporter" - a user who actively shares news stories, crime info or testimony.Site contributor. 
  ex. Someone who is avid about bringing awareness to an issue affecting them or their community.
"Lead Detective" — a user who's post or information has led to a case or issue being resolved. 
  ex. A user leaves a comment which get verified as true. 
  neighborhood sightings with official arrests to help their community stay informed and vigilant. 

## Why This Project:
I love journalism, true-crime stories, and have a passion for bringing justice to victims of crimes and their families. I lost a close friend to gun violence and endured the pain and uncertainty of not knowing who was responsible for almost a year. This project serves to act as an outlet and resource for anyone in a similar situation, looking for answers, guidance, and community.
