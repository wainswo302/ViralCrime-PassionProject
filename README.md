# ViralCrime-PassionProject
www.viralcrime.com

## Elevator Pitch:
ViralCrime.com is a decentralized crime-intelligence platform that archives and verifies local crime incidents that fall through the cracks of mainstream media. It is for victims, families, and neighborhood advocates who need a high-visibility, an AI-powered megaphone to bring awareness to overlooked cases.

## Problem Statement:
Mainstream outlets prioritize sensationalism, leaving thousands of "quiet" crimes (unsolved burglaries, cold cases, and local scams) without public pressure or documentation. This site exists to provide data equity, ensuring that the lack of a news van doesn't mean a lack of justice or exposure, while using AI to verify user-submitted evidence against official municipal records.
